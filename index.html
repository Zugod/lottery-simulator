How hard is it to win the lottery?
Winning the lottery jackpot is far harder than most people imagine. Even if you play regularly for many years, your chance of hitting the jackpot usually stays extremely small. This simulator helps show the reality of lottery odds by running random simulations and probability calculations.
FAQ

No. Lucky numbers have exactly the same probability as random numbers.

Because jackpot odds are extremely small. Even playing for decades usually gives only a tiny chance.
